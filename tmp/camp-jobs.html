Camp Tasks

Office Staff

We are expanding the "camp librarian" position, asking volunteers to run the office during open hours by helping people with:

  • searching the library and signing out music
  • using the photocopier
  • distributing extra blankets, pillows, and toilet paper
  • signing out laundry cards

After the first full day, most of this time will be idle. Feel free to use this time to add bar numbers to your music, check email, practice yoga, etc.

Time:
  • 20 minute training session on camp Sunday
  • a daily shift (camp Monday to Friday) of up to 1 hour

Ideally, we would like to have 6 volunteers, and have the following "office hours" on Monday to Friday:

  • 7:00 - 7:40am (overlapping early breakfast)
  • 8:10 - 8:45am (overlapping breakfast and session 1)
  • 10:40 - 11:00am (snack)
  • 2 - 3pm
  • 6:50 - 7:15pm (overlapping dinner)
  • 8 - 8:45pm

In addition, we would like 1 or 2 volunteers to staff the office on: - the final Saturday, 11am - 12:45

Porters

There's a lot of physical set-up to do on the first Sunday (and undone on the following Saturday):

  • move tables in the classrooms
  • carry electronic keyboards to rooms and set them up
  • move instruments from AC 323 to 3rd floor library
  • move children's instruments
  • move chairs

If you're open to being approached to help with any of these tasks, please sign up! The more people we have, the easier it will be to distribute these tasks.

You will always have the opportunity to decline a particular task (e.g., "I have a rehearsal now", "I need to take care of my kids", "I'm just too tired at the moment"). Signing up simply means "I don't mind if I get asked to help with tasks".

Physical requirements:

  • ability to walk unaided
  • ability to lift 10 pounds (some tasks require more strength, but there's plenty of "light-weight" tasks as well!)

Time:

  • on Sunday and Saturday: anywhere from 15 minutes to 6 hours
  • during the week: 5-10 minutes (e.g., moving a stack of 10 chairs between MPR and 3rd floor library, setting up a keyboard for an outside mini-concert, etc.)
Outside signs at Quest

New campers like having signs to help them find Quest university, and specific buildings on campus when they first arrive.

Time expected:
  • 1 hour on the first camp Sunday
  • 30 minutes on camp Monday
Tasks:
  • Store these signs year-round. Large road signs are 2 meters by 1 meter (approx?), not including 1 meter wire legs. Campus signs are 1 meter by 0.5 meters.
  • On Sunday morning (before noon), bring signs to camp, then place:
    • 3 large road signs, directing traffic to Quest. Locations:
      • https://www.google.ca/maps/place/49%C2%B044'06.3%22N+123%C2%B007'24.8%22W
      • https://www.google.ca/maps/place/49%C2%B044'24.9%22N+123%C2%B006'59.6%22W
      • https://www.google.ca/maps/place/49%C2%B044'25.1%22N+123%C2%B006'36.8%22W
    • 6-8 campus signs directing traffic to registration, and where to park.
  • On Monday, remove the signs, and store for the year.
Inside signs at Quest

It's useful to have signs to help campers navigate Quest university and to show how each room is being used during the week. This includes the sign-up sheets.

Time expected:
  • 4 hours on the first camp Sunday
Tasks:
  • Print signs and fill in any details that are necessary (i.e. room schedules).
  • Put up signs outside the rooms.
Post-Concert Music Sorters

After the final concert, there's usually 8-12 volunteers sorting out the large ensemble music. Each person takes a box of unsorted music (e.g., all the orchestra parts for the large choral work) and sorts it in the order the parts appear in the score.

Time:
  • 1 hour after the final Saturday concert
Evening Snack

We put out a snack after the evening activities, so we need some volunteers to help. We're hoping to get 3-4 people for preparation and/or clean-up for every evening.

Tasks:
  • Prepare plates/bowls of fruit, cookies, chips, baked goods. (We will have the food available)
  • Prepare the drinks
  • Prepare the napkins, cups, etc.
  • Move food&drink from the preparation area to the snacking area.
  • Clean up afterwards, including washing the bowls and serving trays.
Time:
  • 30 minutes for prep and/or 30 minutes for clean-up.
